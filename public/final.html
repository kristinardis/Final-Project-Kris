The Pathway to Optimal Health and Vitality

About this event

This 1 1/2 hour session includes a variety of exercises for both the beginning and the more advanced practioner.
You should feel revitalized and relaxed after even modest amounts of participation.
You should bring a mat and wear comfortable clothing. We recommend that you come prepared to be barefoot.

Schedules

We will be schedule classes each week on a Monday at 6:30 PM weather permitting.
There wil be a warm up period shortly before, and a social hour afterwords
Among the poses we will be demonstrating are the downward facing dog, lotus, bow cat, and child poses.

Teachers

All sessions will be instructed by Swami Ummagumma, renowned speaker and yoga authority
